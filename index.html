Shedy

Booking platform for service providers and clients.

About

Shedy is a platform that allows service providers such as barbers, stylists, and other professionals to manage their appointments and accept bookings from clients.

How it works

  • Providers create a profile and list their services
  • Clients browse and book available time slots
  • Payments are processed securely through Stripe
